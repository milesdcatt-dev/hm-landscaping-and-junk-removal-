08FAQ

Common questions.

How do I get a quote?

Text photos of the junk, yard, or project area to 202-999-7885. We send back a real, written quote, usually within the hour.

What areas do you serve?

Northern Virginia, generally within about 40 minutes of our base. That includes Herndon, Reston, Vienna, Oakton, Oak Hill, Fairfax, Chantilly, Centreville, Great Falls, McLean, Sterling, Ashburn, and surrounding neighborhoods.

How fast can you come out?

Same-day or next-day is often possible depending on the schedule. Text us and we'll let you know the soonest we can get there.

How is pricing decided?

By the job, not by the hour. Price depends on load size, weight, stairs, distance, dump fees, and how much cleanup is needed. You see the number before we start.

What can't you take?

We handle most household and yard items. For hazardous materials or anything unusual, text a photo first and we'll let you know whether we can haul it.

Is there a minimum job size?

No job is too small. Whether it's one mattress or a full garage, send a photo and we'll quote it fairly.
